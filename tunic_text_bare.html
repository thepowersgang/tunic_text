Input mode (Use ` to switch): Strokes Glyphs Verbatim Text (~)
 
 

 
Help:
  • Press the listed keys to toggle the specified stroke
  • The middle stroke is automatically filled if the top-middle or bottom-middle stroke is present
  • Press "space" to commit the current character and move to inserting after the current position
  • A blank character becomes a space (i.e. press space twice to insert a blank space)
  • Two blank characters in a row create a new line (i.e. press space three times)
  • Arrow keys move the insertion cursor (and provide a zoom on the selected character)
  • Backspace and delete will either remove the previous/next character (if inserting) or the current character (if editing)
  • There's no undo, so be careful.
  • To save your text input, copy the `#` part of the URL
  • To quickly clear, just delete the `#` part of the URL and refresh
